2. Salary and Commission

  • The basic salary is $800 with no deductions.
  • Salaries will be paid in full every 15th of the month.
  • Commission is earned after depositing $4,000:
    • 8% commission up to $10,000.
    • 10% commission from $10,000 to $20,000.
    • 15% commission from $20,000 to $50,000.
    • 18% commission above $50,000.
  • If an employee meets their target in the first month, their salary will be increased to $1,000 for the next two months.

3. Rules and Regulations

  • Do not share your location with anyone in Cambodia, especially not with local residents.
  • Do not drink alone with Cambodian girls for the first two months.
  • Do not log into any work-related systems (e.g., Telegram, WhatsApp) on personal devices.

  • Work hours are from 4 PM to 4 AM, with a total of 12 working hours.
  • Employees are allowed a 2-hour break, which can be divided into two 1-hour breaks.

5. Team Incentives and Bonuses

6. Meals Provided

  • Lunch and dinner include rice and vegetables, or other local dishes.

8. Target

  • Daily Target: Each employee must ADD at least 5 customers per day.
  • Weekly Target: Each employee is responsible for introducing at least 10 customers within a week.
  • Your performance will be reviewed weekly to ensure targets are being met.
